### Easy360Viewer Privacy Policy
Thank you for using Easy360Viewer.
This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

This app does not collect any of the following:
- Personal information such as name or email address
- Location data
- Usage or analytics data
- Advertising identifiers

This app requires the following permission:
- **Photo Library**: Used to select 360° photos to display within the app
All data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to external servers.
